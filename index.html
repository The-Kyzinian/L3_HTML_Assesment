HHS Drama Department


The Henderson High School Drama Department provides a comprehensive arts education aligned with the NCEA curriculum, focusing on developing practical skills and building student confidence.

Students engage in both scripted and devised drama, with junior courses establishing fundamental techniques like voice, movement, and dramatic conventions. The senior program progresses to advanced performance skills, knowledge of specific theatre forms, and the exploration of drama practitioners. The department actively participates in co-curricular activities like the annual senior dramatic play and Showquest, offering numerous opportunities for students to build stage presence, technical skills (lighting, sound), and vital soft skills like collaboration and self-management.
