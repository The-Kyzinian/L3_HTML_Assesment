HHS Drama Department



The Henderson High School Drama Department presents a showcase of student talent! These events feature performances across all year levels, from NCEA assessment productions and devised works to the major annual Senior Dramatic Play. Join us for evenings of compelling theatre that highlight students' mastery of dramatic techniques, script analysis, and collaborative stagecraft.
